## 1. Brand Overview

### Who We Are

Technijian was founded in November 2000 by Ravi Jain with a mission to deliver technology support for small to midsize businesses. Over 25 years, that single-consultant operation has grown into a full-spectrum IT services company with offices in the United States (Irvine, California) and India, serving businesses across Orange County and nationwide.

Today, Technijian provides managed IT services, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, AI-driven solutions, compliance support, and custom software development — all backed by 24/7 support through our dedicated "Technijians" pod model.

### What We Offer

Our services are organized into six core pillars:

- **Managed IT Services** — Desktop support, server support, VoIP systems, network maintenance, and 24/7 monitoring
- **Cybersecurity** — CrowdStrike-powered threat protection, continuous monitoring, incident response, and breach prevention
- **Cloud Solutions** — Private cloud (own datacenter), public cloud (AWS, Microsoft 365), SD-WAN, and hybrid environments
- **Compliance & Business Continuity** — HIPAA, PCI, SOC 2, GDPR frameworks, documentation, disaster recovery planning
- **Software Development & AI** — .NET 8, React, WordPress, AI-driven development, CoPilot integration, DevOps, SEO services
- **Strategic IT Consulting** — CTO-as-a-Service, data analytics (Power BI), IT assessments, and technology roadmapping

### Key Differentiators

- **25+ years** of continuous operation serving SMBs and mid-market enterprises
- **The Technijians Pod Model** — A dedicated team assigned to your business who knows your infrastructure inside and out, eliminating the fragmented MSP experience
- **24/7 Global Support** — U.S.-based technicians supplemented by our India office for round-the-clock coverage at no extra cost
- **Cybersecurity-First Approach** — Security is embedded in everything we do, not bolted on as an afterthought
- **AI-Forward Innovation** — We don't just support technology; we help clients leverage AI, automation, and modern development practices to stay ahead

## 3. Brand Personality & Voice

### Brand Archetypes

If Technijian were a person, these archetypes describe who we are:

- **Trusted Advisor** — Knowledgeable, dependable, always available, never condescending
- **Proactive Partner** — Anticipates problems before they arise, invested in your success
- **Innovative Builder** — Embraces AI, modern development, and emerging technologies while keeping solutions practical

### Voice & Tone

Technijian's voice is **down-to-earth and non-corporate**. We are a knowledgeable friend offering clear, helpful guidance — never talking over someone's head or using jargon for its own sake.

**Our tone is:**

- Friendly, but not unprofessional
- Informative, but not authoritative
- Smart, but not complicated
- Confident, but not showy
- Proactive, but not pushy

### Writing Guidelines by Channel

**Website copy:** Lead with the client's challenge, then present our solution. Keep sentences direct. Avoid buzzword stacking (e.g., don't write "synergistic end-to-end digital transformation solutions").

**Blog & podcast content:** Educational and conversational. Establish expertise through practical advice, not self-promotion. Include real-world examples and actionable takeaways.

**Social media:** Concise and accessible. Focus on one idea per post. Use the brand's conversational tone. Avoid all-caps, excessive hashtags, or clickbait phrasing.

**Sales proposals & contracts:** Professional but warm. Clear scope definitions, transparent pricing, no hidden terms. Mirror the "no surprises" promise of our service delivery.

**Email marketing:** Subject lines that communicate value, not hype. Body copy should respect the reader's time — get to the point and include a clear call to action.

## 4. Visual Identity

### Logo

The Technijian logo consists of the multi-colored dot grid icon alongside the "TECHNIJIAN" wordmark with the tagline "technology as a solution" beneath it.

**Usage rules:**

- Minimum display size: 3 inches wide (proportionally scaled)
- Use the standard logo on light backgrounds
- Use the reverse (white) logo on dark backgrounds — dark grey or dark grey overlaid images are preferred
- Never remove, add, rotate, stretch, or resize individual elements
- Never alter kerning, cover up portions, or deviate from approved versions
- Maintain designated clearspace around all sides of the logo (free of text and other graphics)

**Approved formats:** Standard (full color on light), Reverse (white on dark), Icon only (dot grid mark)

### Color Palette

### Typography

**Primary typeface:** Open Sans (Google Fonts)

| Weight | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| Open Sans Bold | Headlines, section headers, CTAs |
| Open Sans Regular | Body text, subheads, descriptions |
| Open Sans Light | Supporting text, captions, metadata |

**Hierarchy:**

- Headlines: Open Sans Bold, title case
- Subheads: Open Sans Regular
- Body text: Open Sans Regular
- Captions and metadata: Open Sans Light

**Alignment:** Text should always be left-justified. Avoid center and right justification except for specific design applications (centered hero text, right-aligned dates).

**Digital sizing guidelines:**

- H1 (page titles): 36–48px
- H2 (section titles): 24–32px
- H3 (subsection): 18–24px
- Body: 16px
- Small/caption: 12–14px

## 5. Photography & Graphics

### Photography Style

Images should portray Technijian's services, technology environments, and the people behind the work. Two approved treatments:

**Dark Tech Aesthetic** (hero sections, title slides, landing pages):
1. Start with a relevant technology image (servers, networking, circuit boards)
2. Desaturate the image
3. Darken and apply a black overlay at 50% opacity
4. Use for backgrounds with white or orange text overlaid

**Light People-Focused** (about pages, testimonials, team profiles):
- Bright, naturally lit photographs
- Show real people in professional settings
- Convey warmth, approachability, and competence

### Graphics & Icons

Graphics should adhere to the approved color palette. Use flat or semi-flat icon styles that complement the modern, clean aesthetic. Avoid overly decorative or 3D-rendered graphics.

When displaying data or processes, prefer clean diagrams and infographics over dense charts.

### Third-Party Logos

- **Single logo:** May be displayed in full color
- **Multiple logos together:** Convert all to greyscale for visual consistency
- Use third-party logos sparingly and only when necessary to represent partnerships or integrations

### Screenshot & Product Images

When screenshots of products or platforms are needed, place them within a device mockup (monitor, laptop, or phone frame) for a polished presentation. For detailed views where a mockup would obscure content, full-width screenshots are acceptable.

## 6. Digital Brand Applications

### Website

The website (technijian.com) is the primary expression of the Technijian brand. All pages should follow the approved typography, color palette, and image guidelines. Key elements include the navigation structure reflecting our six service pillars, prominent CTAs in Core Orange, and consistent use of the Technijians pod model messaging.

### Social Media

Maintain consistent profile images (logo icon) and cover images across all platforms (LinkedIn, X, Facebook). Post graphics should use the approved color palette with Core Orange or Core Blue as dominant colors. Include the Technijian wordmark or icon on all branded graphics.

### Email Marketing

Email templates should feature the full Technijian logo in the header, use Open Sans typography, and include Core Blue for headers with Core Orange for CTAs. Footer should contain contact information, social links, and the Irvine office address.

### Podcast & Video

Episode artwork should follow the dark tech aesthetic with the Technijian logo prominently placed. Video thumbnails should include the episode title in Open Sans Bold with the brand's color palette. Maintain visual consistency across all episodes.

### Sales Collateral

Proposals, one-pagers, and data sheets should follow the print guidelines: 8.5" × 11" format, left-justified text, approved color palette, and the Technijian logo in the header or footer. Use the dark header/light content "sandwich" pattern for multi-page documents.

## 7. Content & Messaging Framework

### AI Services Messaging

When communicating about Technijian's AI capabilities, follow these principles:

- Lead with the business outcome, not the technology
- Be specific about what AI enables (e.g., "AI-powered development that reduces delivery time by 40%") rather than vague promises
- Avoid hype language ("revolutionary," "game-changing," "disruptive") — use confident, grounded language instead
- When referencing AI platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, CoPilot), focus on how Technijian integrates and applies them for client benefit

### Compliance Messaging

Given our work in regulated industries (healthcare, finance, education), compliance messaging should be:

- Specific about frameworks (name HIPAA, PCI, SOC 2, GDPR explicitly)
- Confident but not guaranteeing outcomes ("we help you navigate compliance" not "we guarantee compliance")
- Focused on risk reduction and peace of mind

### SEO Content Guidelines

Blog posts and web content should balance SEO optimization with readability. Write for humans first, search engines second. Include relevant keywords naturally — never stuff or force keyword placement. Every piece of content should provide genuine value to the reader.
