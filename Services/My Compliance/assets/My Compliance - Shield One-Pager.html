Why Shield
  • Endpoints Leak Data: Regulated data (PHI, CUI, PII, PCI) walks out via USB, email, paste, and screen — Shield catches it.
  • Block, Don't Just Watch: Real-time block/warn actions stop exfiltration before it completes — not just alerts after.
  • Insider Risk, Quantified: Per-user risk scores let security teams focus on the few users that matter most.
  • Evidence for the Auditor: Every DLP event, exception, and incident is logged in formats auditors accept directly.
  • Session Playback: Investigations get definitive answers — see what the user actually did, not just metadata.
