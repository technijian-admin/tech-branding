Why Audit
  • Evidence Is the Audit: Every framework's audit hinges on evidence. Without systematic collection, you scramble the week before.
  • Linked, Not Loose: Evidence is attached to controls — assessors see traceability, not a folder of PDFs.
  • Audit Season → Non-Event: Pre-built packs mean auditors get what they need on day one, not week three.
  • Tamper-Evident Trail: Append-only logs and immutable exports prove integrity to assessors and regulators.
  • Multi-Framework Reuse: One evidence repository serves HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI, ISO, CMMC, GDPR, NIST, and HITRUST.
