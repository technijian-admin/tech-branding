Vault Capabilities
Credential Management
  • Password vault, API keys, tokens, certificates, TOTP seeds
  • Per-tenant isolation — no cross-client exposure
  • Structured documentation linked to credentials
  • Browser, mobile, and ScreenConnect injection
Rotation & Lifecycle
  • Scheduled and on-demand credential rotation
  • Verification & rollback on rotation failure
  • Failure workflows with automated re-try & alerts
  • Aging & expiration tracking with renewal reminders
Access Control
  • Role-based access with least-privilege defaults
  • Just-in-time (JIT) leases for time-bound access
  • Step-up authentication for privileged retrieval
  • Approval workflows for sensitive credential checkout
Audit & Hygiene
  • Privileged access logging — who, what, when
  • Weak & reused password detection
  • Credential aging & orphaned-account reports
  • Framework-aligned audit exports (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI)
