Why Govern
  • Policies Are Half the Audit: Auditors test the policy, then test that you follow it — both must hold up
  • Lifecycle Is the Failure Point: Written-once-and-ignored policies are worse than no policies
  • Exceptions Done Right: Formal exception process with compensating controls keeps you defensible
  • Evidence Linkage: Every control maps to a policy and to evidence — no audit-prep scramble
  • Nexus Migration Path: Policies authored today flow into Nexus Govern automation tomorrow
