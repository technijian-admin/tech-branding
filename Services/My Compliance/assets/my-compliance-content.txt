# Technijian My Compliance — IT Compliance Services

## Overview
My Compliance is Technijian's comprehensive IT compliance service suite that helps organizations achieve and maintain compliance with major frameworks including HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, GDPR, NIST CSF, CIS Controls, CMMC, ISO 27001, and more.

Today we deliver these services using best-in-class commercial tools combined with 25+ years of compliance expertise. We're building Technijian Nexus — our own purpose-built multi-tenant compliance automation platform — to unify everything into one integrated system with AI-native workflows.

## Service Modules (Modular / À la carte)

### Available Today via Commercial Tools + Technijian Expertise
1. **My Compliance — Vault** (credential & secrets management)
   - Today: N-able Passportal
   - Password rotation, privileged access, secure storage, audit logging
   - Future (Nexus): Nexus Vault module with JIT leases, integrated workflows

2. **My Compliance — Assess** (vulnerability & security assessments)
   - Today: Network Detective by RapidFire Tools (internal network + AD) + Pentest-Tools.com (external pentest + attack surface)
   - Internal vulnerability assessments, AD audits, external scans, cloud posture, BCDR reviews
   - Future (Nexus): Nexus Assess module (already productized as Nexus Assess + Pulse)

3. **My Compliance — Shield** (endpoint DLP + user activity monitoring)
   - Today: Teramind
   - Data loss prevention, insider threat detection, activity recording, OCR search
   - Future (Nexus): Nexus Shield module with integrated DLP

### Available Today via Technijian Consulting (Manual Delivery)
4. **My Compliance — Atlas** (documentation & SOPs)

   - Future (Nexus): Automated knowledge base with AI Q&A

5. **My Compliance — Discover** (IT inventory & asset discovery)
   - Today: RMM extracts + Network Detective reports + manual tracking

6. **My Compliance — Govern** (policy & procedure lifecycle)
   - Today: Policies built manually from framework templates; approvals via email

7. **My Compliance — Audit** (audit logging & evidence)
   - Today: Evidence binders built manually from tool exports + spreadsheets

8. **My Compliance — Signal** (SIEM & detection engineering)
   - Today: 3rd-party SIEM + manual tuning + manual triage

9. **My Compliance — Guardian** (file protection & FIM)
