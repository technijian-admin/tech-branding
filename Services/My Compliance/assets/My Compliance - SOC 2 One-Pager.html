Why SOC 2 Now
  • Enterprise procurement requires it: No SOC 2 report, no contract — deals stall at security review
  • Cyber insurance demands it: Carriers increasingly require SOC 2 for coverage and renewal
  • Points of Focus expanded: AICPA raised the evidentiary bar — old control playbooks no longer satisfy
  • Type II is the de facto standard: Type I only buys 6-12 months to get the real report
  • Engineering shouldn’t run this: Managed SOC 2 programs protect velocity and deliver cleaner audits
