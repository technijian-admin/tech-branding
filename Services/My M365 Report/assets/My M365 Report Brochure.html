How It Works
Setup to Delivery

Connected in Under 2 Business Days.
Reports Every Month — Automatically.

My M365 Report connects via a read-only Azure AD App Registration using Microsoft Graph API. No agents, no on-prem software, no MX record changes. We never write to your tenant.
1
App Registration
We provide a 15-minute guide to create a Global Reader service account in your Entra ID — read-only, no admin write access needed.
2
Upload Brand Kit
Send us your logo (PNG, 600px+) and primary/secondary hex color codes. We template every report to your MSP’s brand.
3
Daily Collection
We pull your M365 data via Graph API every 24 hours — user activity, license assignments, security events, storage metrics.
4
Monthly Delivery
On the 1st of each month, a branded Word report is generated, QC’d, and delivered to your designated inbox.
