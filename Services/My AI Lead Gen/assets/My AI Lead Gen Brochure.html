The Pipeline
Four production stages — each self-healing, scheduled, and tuned to your ICP — running on autopilot every day and week.

Harvest

Ingest from 10+ public & private sources on daily/weekly cadence.
  • SAM.gov & USASpending federal contracts
  • SBA 504 / 7(a) loan data, state DOT awards
  • County permits, UCC filings, clerk-recorder
  • City building permits & plan-check data
  • HOA ARC, story poles, just-sold properties

Enrich

AI + API enrichment to build full business, property & contact records.
  • Apollo + BatchData contact & role discovery
  • ATTOM property, Spokeo ownership lookup
  • Claude deep search for hidden leads
  • Firmographics, tech stack, spend signals
  • Geocoding, HOA match, architect-of-record

Score

ICP-fit, dedup, intent ranking — ready for prioritized outreach.
  • Client-fit scoring vs. your ICP definition
  • Identity resolution & cross-source dedup
  • Tier 1 / 2 / 3 auto-categorization
  • Recency-weighted intent decay
  • Self-healing pipelines with learning loop

Deliver

Outreach-ready artifacts drop in your inbox, CRM, or Obsidian vault.
  • Enriched CSVs for HubSpot / Salesforce / Pipedrive
  • Personalized email drafts (HTML + plain text)
  • Weekly DOCX + PDF lead reports for leadership
  • Obsidian vault sync for research context
  • Optional SDR handoff & cadence automation
