The Continuity Challenge
Most organizations think they have a backup strategy — until ransomware hits, a datacenter goes dark, or an auditor asks to see the restore test evidence. Then the gaps get expensive.
🔒

Ransomware Can’t Be Negotiated

Modern ransomware targets your backups first. Without immutable storage and air-gapped copies, you are paying the ransom — and hoping the decryption key works.

Immutable, air-gapped Veeam backups

Untested Backups = No Backups

Quarterly restore tests with evidence

RPO/RTO Mismatch to Business

Your backup frequency does not match what the business can tolerate losing; your restore speed does not match what the business can tolerate waiting through.

RPO/RTO-tied backup & DR architecture

Compliance Evidence Gaps

HIPAA, PCI, and SOC 2 auditors ask for backup retention, restore test evidence, and offsite copies. Most organizations cannot produce these documents on demand.

Audit-ready evidence binders

Why My Continuity Now?

  • Ransomware targeting of backup infrastructure has doubled year-over-year — attackers encrypt your backups before they encrypt production
  • Cyber insurance carriers now require immutable backups, documented restore testing, and offsite copies as conditions of coverage and renewal
  • HIPAA, SOC 2, and PCI require documented recovery procedures, retention policies, and evidence of testing — not just backup software licenses
  • Hardware failure, human error, and accidental deletion still account for the majority of data loss incidents — resilience is not optional
