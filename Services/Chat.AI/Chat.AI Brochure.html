The Challenge with Enterprise AI Today
Organizations face real barriers when adopting AI at scale — from vendor lock-in to security gaps that stall innovation and erode trust.

Single-Vendor Lock-In

Most enterprise AI tools tie you to a single model provider. When that provider changes pricing, degrades quality, or suffers downtime, your entire organization is impacted with no fallback.

➜
Multi-provider orchestration across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Azure — switch models instantly without workflow disruption.

No Cost Visibility

AI spend is a black box. Finance teams can't attribute costs to departments, projects, or individuals. Budgets spiral without warning, and there's no way to optimize spend per use case.

➜
Per-call cost attribution with department-level budgets, real-time dashboards, and automatic spend alerts before limits are reached.

Hallucinations & Unreliable Output

Single-model responses hallucinate facts, invent citations, and produce inconsistent answers. In regulated industries, one wrong answer can trigger compliance violations.

➜
AI Council consensus — multiple models deliberate anonymously, cross-check facts, and produce a synthesized answer with higher reliability.

Security & Compliance Gaps

Consumer AI tools lack tenant isolation, audit trails, and role-based access. Sensitive data leaks between users. Meeting HIPAA, SOC 2, or GDPR requirements is impossible.

➜
Row-level security + full audit trails with Azure AD SSO, RBAC roles, and data isolation that satisfies enterprise compliance frameworks.
