The Problem with Enterprise File Storage
Why File Storage Is Broken for Growing Teams

Most businesses piece together file storage from consumer-grade tools or overpay for bloated enterprise platforms that charge per gigabyte, per user, and per feature. The result: unpredictable costs, compliance risk, and data scattered across platforms your team doesn’t control.

💵
Unpredictable Overage Bills
Box, Dropbox, and OneDrive charge per GB above your plan tier. A single large project can spike your bill by hundreds of dollars mid-month — with no warning.
🔒
Per-Seat Feature Tax
Box, Dropbox, and OneDrive charge premium tiers for basic features: E2E encryption, audit logs, SAML SSO, advanced sharing controls. Getting everything an enterprise needs often means the $30+/user Enterprise plan.
📹
Weak Tenant Isolation
Shared consumer-grade infrastructure means noisy-neighbor effects, shared policy defaults, and cross-customer incident blast radius. Audit logs may not cover admin actions across tenants.
🔍
Vendor Lock-In
Proprietary platforms make migration painful. Your files, permissions, share links, and workflows are all tangled in a closed system you can never fully audit or own.
⚠️
Compliance Blind Spots
HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2 require audit trails, data residency control, and encryption configuration that most consumer cloud platforms don’t provide out of the box.
📱
Poor Mobile & Offline Experience
Field teams, remote workers, and executives need seamless offline access. Most platforms throttle mobile sync or require premium add-ons for offline file access on all devices.

Security & Compliance
Enterprise Security Built In — Not Bolted On

My Disk ships with a comprehensive security stack configured out of the box. No extra add-ons required. Every layer is auditable, configurable, and HIPAA/GDPR-ready from day one.

🔒 Encryption at Rest
AES-256 server-side encryption on all stored files. Optional client-side (E2EE) encryption for maximum privacy — only your device holds the keys. Files are encrypted before they leave your machine.
🔐 Encryption in Transit
All connections use TLS 1.3 with modern cipher suites. HSTS enforced. Perfect forward secrecy enabled. SSL certificates auto-renewed via Let’s Encrypt or your own CA.
👁 Two-Factor & SSO
TOTP authenticator app, FIDO2/WebAuthn hardware keys, and enterprise SAML/OIDC SSO. Enforce 2FA by user group or across the entire instance via admin policy.
📋 Audit Logs & Activity
Every file access, download, share, login, and admin action is logged with timestamp, user, and IP address. Export logs to SIEM. Retained 90 days by default, configurable.
🔄 File Versioning & Recovery
30-day file version history with per-file restore. Recycle bin with configurable retention. Daily encrypted backups with point-in-time recovery by Technijian.
⛚ Brute Force Protection
Automatic IP throttling after failed login attempts. Account lockout policies, suspicious login notifications, and geographic access restrictions per user or group.
