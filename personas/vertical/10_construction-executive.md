## 5. Pain Points & Challenges

### Top operational pains (ranked)

1. **Luxury buyer lead-gen is inconsistent.** Relies on realtor referrals and a stale website. Peer builders running Facebook / Instagram / AI-generated campaigns; he's behind.
2. **Site / trailer Wi-Fi** and connectivity is patchy. Superintendent can't open a RFI on site.
3. **Plan files everywhere.** Revit / PDF on SharePoint, email, personal Dropbox, site tablet. Which is canonical?
4. **Buyer portals / deposit collection** — buyers want online visibility; his website is a brochure.
5. **Endpoint theft risk** — foreman laptops, site tablets stolen from trailers regularly; no MDM or remote wipe.
6. **Sub onboarding** — new subs need file access; provisioning takes days.
7. **Accounting + PM systems disconnected** — Sage 300 vs Procore; weekly reconciliation.
8. **Azure AD vs on-prem AD confusion** — hybrid identity not cleanly done.
9. **Construction-side phishing / invoice fraud** — wire transfer redirect attempts common.
10. **Change-order chaos** — emails, paper, verbal; revenue leakage.

### Biggest fears / worst outcomes

- Buyer data breach → litigation (HNW buyers are litigious).
- Wire-fraud on a $2M closing.
- OSHA event with digital records missing or tampered.
- Project plans leaked to competitor.
- Ransomware hitting during active closings.
- Key project manager walks with project files.

### Current workarounds / status quo

- Marketing agency posts Instagram for him monthly.
- File server "mapped drive" on every workstation.
- Personal email for "I couldn't get on VPN today."
- Paper change orders signed on site.

### Gaps in current providers / tools

- No endpoint management or MDM.
- No 24/7 IT.
- No site-specific IT services (most MSPs stop at office).
- No marketing-side SEO / AI lead-gen.

## 9. Objections & Pushbacks

1. **"Builders don't need fancy IT."**
   - **Why:** Cultural bias.
   - **Reframe:** Wire-fraud, plan leakage, and buyer litigation make IT operational risk material. Show peer-builder case.

2. **"Procore is all the software I need."**
   - **Why:** Overtrust in vertical SaaS.
   - **Reframe:** Procore doesn't do endpoint, email, identity, or SEO. Complement, don't replace.

3. **"My guy handles IT."**
   - **Why:** One-person MSP loyalty.
   - **Reframe:** Co-managed — he keeps the IT guy, we add 24/7 + cloud + security.

4. **"AI lead-gen is hype."**
   - **Why:** Burned by generic leads.
   - **Reframe:** Show a sample enriched luxury-buyer list from My AI Lead Gen; reference Case Studies/Construction/Construction - AI Lead Generation for Luxury Home Builder.pdf.

5. **"Site Wi-Fi is the trailer's problem."**
   - **Why:** Culture of field independence.
   - **Reframe:** One seamless network = one RFI workflow, faster closings.

6. **"We don't have Azure AD problems."**
   - **Why:** He doesn't know what Azure AD is.
   - **Reframe:** Show the case of hybrid AD cleanup with datacenter migration (Case Studies/Construction/Construction - Datacenter Migration & Azure AD Hybrid.pdf).
