### San Clemente competitors (new brand's market)

| Competitor | Market | Reviews | Hours | Weakness |
|------------|--------|---------|-------|----------|
| Rim Revival Mobile Wheel Restoration | San Clemente, mobile | 68 Yelp | Mon–Sun 8am–5pm | Only 68 reviews — brand is NOT dominant |
| Fix Auto San Clemente | San Clemente, shop | — | — | Autobody primary; wheels are secondary |

### Gap analysis

**The white space Monaco + San Clemente WR can own:**

1. **Weekend availability**: Monaco is closed Sat/Sun; Rim Revival closes at 5pm. A brand that offers 7-day mobile service captures all weekend car-care moments (detailing day, pre-road trip, post-weekend damage).

2. **Luxury / Tesla specialist positioning**: OC has one of the highest Tesla per-capita rates in California. No competitor has staked out "Tesla wheel specialist" explicitly. Monaco's precision work with expensive forged wheels (per Yelp) positions them for this.

3. **Review velocity dominance in San Clemente**: Rim Revival has 68 reviews. Monaco Costa Mesa has 222. Systematically generating reviews for San Clemente WR could achieve #1 Yelp/Google Map Pack ranking within 90 days.

4. **Dealer & fleet partnerships**: New car dealers in both markets deal with transport damage, lot damage, test-drive curb rash constantly. No competitor appears to be actively courting dealer wholesale accounts.

5. **AI-native booking experience**: No competitor appears to have an AI-powered photo-to-quote flow. "Text us a photo, get a quote in 5 minutes" is a defensible moat.
