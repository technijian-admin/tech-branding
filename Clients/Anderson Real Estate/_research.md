## 2. GTM Motion — Account-Based + Operations-Heavy (NOT lead-gen)

Anderson is a commercial-real-estate **owner-operator / landlord**. Its "business development" function is two things at once:

1. **Leasing & tenant growth** — fill and hold space; attract the right tenants for the repositioned campus and across 53 properties; retain the 250+ tenant base.
2. **Portfolio operations & efficiency** — run a 3.1M sq ft / 9-market portfolio with a lean family-office team (lease administration, CAM, maintenance, reporting, institutional knowledge).

**Classification: ACCOUNT-BASED (ABM) + INTEGRATION-HEAVY.** Commercial leasing — especially trophy office — is won through **named tenants and the tenant-rep brokers who control deal flow**, relationships, tours, and proposals. This is *not* a broad demand-gen funnel. **Never use "lead generation," "fill your funnel," or "shotgun marketing" language.** Frame AI as the *operating layer under* a relationship-driven, family-owned platform. AI **amplifies** "people come first," it does not replace the human relationship.

This maps cleanly to the skill's **two-front** framing (Growth + Integration & efficiency).

## 3. Market Moment — Why Now (real, citable)

- **Century City is the top-performing office submarket on the West Coast.** Big occupiers are relocating from Downtown LA to Century City; clear **flight-to-quality** dynamic favoring premium, repositioned, amenitized space.
- **Rents:** Century City average base rent ≈ **$80/sf** (Q2 2025), up sharply since 2018 — the highest-rent submarket in the metro by a wide margin (West LA ≈ $5.68/sf/month).
- **Recovery:** West LA office direct vacancy ≈ 15.8% (Q4 2024) but **stabilizing**; Q1 2026 posted the **highest single-quarter leasing total since before the pandemic**; Beverly Hills & Century City lead LA office leasing.
- **Implication:** Anderson's $100M Anderson Towers reposition lands in exactly the right submarket at exactly the right time — but premium space only wins if the firm can **find, court, and convert the right tenants and brokers**, then **operate the campus** at an institutional standard. That is the AI opportunity.

### AI in CRE — the state of play (citable, grounds the pitch)
- **Highest-ROI AI uses in CRE for 2026:** lease abstraction, virtual leasing agents, maintenance triage, portfolio analytics.
- **Lease abstraction:** a human analyst spends **2–3 hours per complex lease**; AI does it in minutes. At 250+ leases this is **1–2 full-time analysts of capacity** recovered without headcount. (e.g., Prophia — AI lease abstraction with source-linked citations.)
- **Adoption gap = first-mover edge:** only **~5% of firms** report achieving most of their AI goals; **60%+** say they are strategically/organizationally/technically unprepared. Proptech VC hit **$16.7B in 2025 (+68% YoY)**.
- **Strategic framing:** the operator's real question is *not* "which point tool do I buy" but "**how do I avoid 14 disconnected tools that each own a fragment of my data**" — i.e., an integrated AI layer, which is Technijian's sweet spot.

## 5. The Customers Anderson Serves — Persona Candidates

For a landlord, the "customers" are **tenants (by asset class) + the brokers who control deal flow**. Evidence supports **5 personas**:

1. **The Trophy Office Tenant** — Century City PE/finance/law/entertainment firm seeking landmark, amenitized space (Ares is the proof). High value, low volume. *Core Blue.*
2. **The Tenant-Rep Broker** — CBRE/JLL/Cushman/Savills broker who controls which buildings a tenant tours. The **gatekeeper** for most commercial deals; relationship + responsiveness win. *Core Orange.*
3. **The Lifestyle / Retail Tenant** — boutique retail & F&B on Montana Ave (Santa Monica), Westwood Village, Brentwood. Brand-fit and foot-traffic matter. *Teal.*
4. **The Industrial / Flex Tenant** — light-industrial / creative users in Oxnard, Ventura County, Thousand Oaks. Practical, price/space-driven. *Dark Charcoal.*
5. **The Renewing Anchor / Long-Term Tenant** — the existing 250+ tenant base. **Retention is far cheaper than new leasing** (saves commissions, downtime, TI). *Gold.*

- Prospect/target intelligence for Anderson Towers — which firms are in-market for Century City space, who their broker is, what they need.

**Front 2 — Portfolio Operations & Efficiency (integration):**
- **Lease abstraction & intelligence** across 250+ leases (the #1 ROI win) — searchable, source-linked, dates/options/CAM terms surfaced automatically.
- **CAM reconciliation & finance automation** — reduce manual spreadsheet reconciliation across 53 properties.
- **Document intelligence** — estoppels, SNDAs, vendor contracts, insurance certs auto-summarized and tracked.
- **Maintenance / work-order triage** — AI-routed tenant requests; predictive maintenance across the engineering team.
- **Portfolio analytics & reporting** — occupancy, rollover/expiration exposure, revenue, anomaly detection — one dashboard, not 14 tools.
- **Tenant experience** — 24/7 AI assistant for tenant requests, building info, after-hours.
- **Institutional knowledge retention** — capture 50 years of family-firm decisions/relationships before they walk out the door (Weaviate + Obsidian pattern).
