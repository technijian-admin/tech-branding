## What They Do

Premium bioskills laboratory and cadaver lab operator serving the medical device industry, healthcare institutions, and surgical training programs.

**Core offerings:**
1. Bioskills labs / cadaver labs — 10,000+ sq ft per location; wet/dry configurations
2. Perfusion and high-fidelity simulation services
3. Medical device development and testing support
4. Surgical training and continuing medical education (CME)
5. OMNIMED SmartOR — AI-enabled telemetry platform for real-time surgical performance analytics
6. Conference/event space (amphitheater 100+ seats, multi-purpose rooms)
