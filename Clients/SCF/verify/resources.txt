FLEET CARD INFORMATION

Fleet managers need a comprehensive fuel solution that provides control over expenses, enhances security against fraud, and delivers detailed insights that drive real savings.

SC Fuels meets these needs with customizable fleet card programs designed to provide complete control over your fueling operations. A dedicated fuel advocate will learn about your business needs and help you develop a tailored fleet card program. Choose from various providers, including CFN, Pacific Pride, and Voyager.

What Our Fleet Card Programs Offer

Here are some of the features our fleet card programs provide.

Enhanced Security and Fraud Protection

Go beyond the limitations of traditional credit cards with security features that safeguard your bottom line:

PIN-protected transactions: Every purchase requires a unique PIN, preventing unauthorized use if a card is lost or stolen.
Cardless swipe app: Your drivers can authorize fuel pumps directly from their mobile devices, protecting them from card skimmers and increasing convenience.
Immediate card deactivation: If a card is lost or stolen, you can instantly deactivate it through the online portal, securing your account in real time.
Real-time e-receipts: Get instant email verification for transactions, providing an immediate record and an extra layer of security and tracking.
Improved Control and Streamlined Reporting

Take command of your fleet’s spending and simplify your administrative workload with powerful management and reporting tools:

Customizable purchase controls: Dictate exactly how, when, and where drivers use fleet cards. Set limits by fuel type, gallons per transaction, time of day, or day of the week to restrict card use to legitimate business purposes only.
Detailed transaction data: Eliminate the hassle of collecting paper receipts. Our point of sale system captures comprehensive data for every transaction, including driver ID, odometer readings, and purchase details, making accounting and expense tracking effortless.
Actionable insights: Use our detailed monthly reports to monitor key metrics like miles per gallon (MPG) and cost per mile. These insights help you identify opportunities for fuel savings, flag potential maintenance issues, and optimize overall fleet efficiency.
Simplified tax reporting: The detailed data captured by our system makes it easier to prepare and file for fuel tax refunds, such as those under the International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA).
Why Trust SC Fuels for Fleet Cards?
