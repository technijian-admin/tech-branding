## 00 — The Question This File Answers (READ FIRST)

> *"They already use My SEO — but can My AI Lead Gen help grow the business? Is that possible?"*

**Short answer: Yes — but for ONE of the two growth engines, not both. Be precise about which, or it backfires.**

- **Consumer patient acquisition (most of the revenue): My AI Lead Gen is the WRONG tool.** You do not — and ethically/legally cannot — cold-prospect individual consumers for an elective facelift out of public databases. Nobody buys $25K facial surgery from a cold outreach. Consumer growth is **inbound demand capture + nurture + reputation**, layered on the My SEO they already run (add GEO, paid social, an on-site consult-booking assistant, and lead nurture).
- **Professional-referral growth (a real, underused channel): My AI Lead Gen is the RIGHT tool — and it's a strong fit.** Facial plastic surgeons get a meaningful share of business from *other practices*. That referral universe (dermatologists, dentists/oral surgeons, OB/GYNs, primary care, optometrists, non-surgical med spas) is finite, **nameable, and harvestable from public data (NPI registry, Google Business)** — exactly what My AI Lead Gen does (Harvest → Enrich → Score → Deliver). The killer line: Dr. Robinson performs **Mohs reconstruction**, so every dermatologist/Mohs surgeon in Orange County is a natural, high-intent referral target.

So: pitch My AI Lead Gen as a **B2B professional-referral engine**, not a consumer-patient engine. That framing is honest, on-model, and genuinely additive to the existing My SEO. (See §04–§05.)

## 04 — Can My AI Lead Gen Help? The Two-Engine Answer

EBRMD has two distinct growth engines. They need different tools. Conflating them is the classic mistake.

### Engine A — Consumer Patient Acquisition (NOT My AI Lead Gen)
The patient is an individual consumer making a high-consideration, personal decision. The motion is **inbound capture + nurture + reputation**:

| Play | Tool | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Rank + get cited for procedure searches | My SEO (live) + **GEO** | Patients search and ask AI before they call |
| Capture the visitor | **Chat.AI** consult assistant + booking funnel | Aesthetics sites bounce; an assistant answers "how much / am I a candidate / financing?" and books the consult |
| **Nurture the consult → surgery** | My AI / My Dev | Patients deliberate for weeks; automated, tasteful email/SMS nurture lifts the #1 conversion metric |
| Reputation engine | My AI | Systematic review requests close the 32-vs-700 gap |
| Paid + social (visual) | (add-on) | Instagram/Meta + paid search are native to aesthetics |

> My AI Lead Gen's outbound-harvest model does **not** apply here — you cannot ethically harvest "people who want a facelift," and cold outreach does not sell elective surgery.

### Engine B — Professional-Referral Growth (YES — My AI Lead Gen fits)
A facial plastic surgeon earns referrals from *other practices*. This universe is finite, public-record-listed, and harvestable — **the exact job My AI Lead Gen is built for** (Harvest → Enrich → Score → Deliver, from NPI/Google Business and other public sources):

| Referral source | Why they refer to a facial plastic surgeon | Priority |
|-----------------|--------------------------------------------|----------|
| **Dermatologists / Mohs surgeons** | **Mohs skin-cancer excisions on the face need reconstruction — Dr. Robinson does Mohs reconstruction.** Direct, high-intent, recurring. | ★★★★★ |
| Non-surgical med spas | Take Botox/filler revenue but refer surgical cases (facelift, eyelid, nose) up | ★★★★☆ |
| Dentists / oral & maxillofacial | Chin/jaw, facial trauma, cosmetic facial referrals | ★★★☆☆ |
| Optometrists / ophthalmology | Droopy-lid (ptosis) and functional/cosmetic blepharoplasty | ★★★☆☆ |
| OB/GYN, primary care, aesthetician/salons | General cosmetic referrals; "mommy makeover" face component | ★★☆☆☆ |

**My AI Lead Gen application (concrete):** harvest every dermatology/Mohs, med-spa, dental, and optometry practice in South OC from public data → enrich (size, services, contact) → score (Mohs derms first) → run account-based outreach in Dr. Robinson's voice (intro, before/after proof, "send me your Mohs reconstructions") → reps/coordinator follow up. This is **account-based**, ethical, on-model, and a channel almost no competitor systematizes.
