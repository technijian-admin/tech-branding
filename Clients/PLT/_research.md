## 5. Market facts (SOURCED — cite as ranges; these are market facts, NOT Pangea's numbers)

- **Luxury travel market ≈ $1.5T+ (2025), ~8% CAGR** (Straits/Grand View cluster — directional).
- **Advisor resurgence (ASTA):** 44% (2021) → **50% more likely to use an advisor now (+14% YoY)**; **68%** say trip planning is more complex now.
- **Virtuoso 2025:** overall sales **+12% YoY**, hotels **+26%**, ultra-luxe cruise (>$50K) **+43%**; **+76%** more consumers seeking advisors via virtuoso.com; **65%** of affluent clients use an advisor for the human "layer of protection."
- **Economics:** commissions — cruises 10–16%, hotels ~10–15%, tours 12–18%, air ~0–2%; **55%+ of advisors now charge planning fees** ($50–$500). Boutique/luxury advisors carry only **~8–12 active planning clients/month** because each trip is time-intensive (the capacity ceiling AI lifts).
- **Manual back-office pain:** itinerary research, multi-supplier quoting, and client-document assembly are highly manual; vendors pitch "minutes instead of hours." A consumer-side proxy puts trip planning at **~20–30 hours** (Journy founder interview — consumer, not an advisor-time study; label directional). No rigorous "advisor hours per luxury itinerary" figure exists — flag any number.

## 6. AI-in-travel trends (SOURCED)

- **59% of advisors now use AI (up from 41% in 2024)**; 57% interested in AI for marketing/content. **2026 = "the year of agentic AI."**
- **>50% of travelers now use AI to plan** (~74% Millennials / 72% Gen Z) — "largest behavioral shift in a decade" (Phocuswright "Search Slips, AI Surges"). Search is losing share to AI as the trip-planning "front door."
- **The clean thesis:** AI removes the manual grunt work (research, quoting, document assembly) so the advisor spends MORE time on the high-touch relationship and selling. The relationship is the moat (65% use an advisor for the human layer); AI is the leverage under it. Discovery channel for affluent clients: **referrals dominate; Instagram is the leading inspiration channel; Google/reviews validate.**
