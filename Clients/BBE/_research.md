## 07 — How GCs Hire Earthwork Subs (Procurement Dynamics)

Commercial earthwork subs win work through a layered procurement funnel:

1. **Pre-qualification relationship** — GC maintains a vetted sub roster; the sub is on the invite list or not
2. **Invite-to-bid** — GC estimator sends scope + plans to 3–5 qualified subs
3. **Competitive bid** — price + schedule + alternates submitted
4. **Award** — usually lowest responsible bid; relationship/track record as tiebreaker
5. **Negotiated / sole-source** — for trusted subs on repeat GC clients; highest margin

**AI creates advantage at steps 1, 2, and 5:**
- Step 1: AI content authority makes BBE the "trusted name" GC estimators already know before the bid invite
- Step 2: AI bid portal monitoring (ConstructConnect, Dodge, PlanetBids) surfaces new projects in BBE's sweet spot 24–48 hours before competitors see them
- Step 5: Account intelligence on named GC principals enables relationship deepening that unlocks negotiated work
