### Industry & market (facts)
- **Recovery/flows:** AF&PA 2024 paper recycling rate **60–64%** (methodology changed 2024 → lower than old figures; never mix); **46M tons** paper recycled 2024; US mills consumed **32.7M tons** recovered fiber (44.4% of furnish). **~27% of US recovered paper exported** (falling 3 straight yrs); OCC consumed mostly domestically.
- **China National Sword:** effective **Jan 1 2018** (0.5% contamination limit Mar 1 2018). OCC ~**$174→$25/ton** (2017→2019); **mixed paper $0 → −$2/ton**. SE-Asia redirection closed fast.
- **Domestic counterweight:** **>8M tons/yr** new 100%-recycled US mill capacity since 2018, ~25 projects (Green Bay Packaging 685k t 2021; Pratt Wapakoneta ~400k t 2019; Cascades Bear Island 465k t 2023; Domtar Kingsport ~600k t 2023; Graphic Packaging K2/Waco). Wave now ~complete.
- **Price benchmark:** **Fastmarkets RISI** (Yellow Sheet→RISI 2012) dominates; regional by freight. ⚠️ **CONTESTED — Feb 2025 PCA & Graphic Packaging publicly began abandoning RISI as a price reference** → opening for transparent alternatives.
- **Volatility:** OCC swung 2–7× within ~24 mo; has not sustainably topped $100/ton since 2022; mixed paper carries negative-price tail risk.

### Regulatory (facts)
- **Basel plastic amendments (in force Jan 1 2021):** triad **B3011 (clean single-resin, exempt) / Y48 (mixed → PIC) / A3210 (hazardous → PIC)**. ⚠️ **US is NOT a Basel Party** → US exporters of controlled (Y48/A3210) plastic are barred from Basel Parties except via **OECD members + Canada + Mexico** arrangements. Clean B3011 moves on ordinary docs. US scrap-plastic exports −12% (2021), −22% (2022).
- **EPR = SEVEN states:** ME (2021), OR (2021, **program LIVE Jul 1 2025**), CO (2022), CA (**SB 54**, regs effective **May 1 2026**), MN (2024), MD (2025), WA (2025). PRO = **Circular Action Alliance** (6 of 7). **Eco-modulation tilts producers toward fiber** (lower fees). RI 2025 = needs-assessment only.
- **ISRI→ReMA rebrand 2024.** Grades by number: **OCC #11, DS OCC #12, DLK #13, mixed #54, SRPN #56, SOP #37, white ledger #40/#41.** Copper: Barley/Berry=No.1, Birch/Cliff=No.2; Zorba=mixed nonferrous, Twitch=Al, Zurik=stainless. (Seed corrections: OCC=#11 not #37; SOP=#37.)
