## What the Free SEO Report Is (know this before you dial)

A custom **SEO Performance Report** Technijian researches and builds for the prospect's specific website — pulling from Semrush and a full technical SEO audit. It is a written report (~15–20 pages) that shows the prospect, in plain language:

| Section | What it shows the prospect |
|---|---|
| **Overall SEO grade** | A simple A–F score of their website's search health |
| **Organic traffic & keywords** | How much search traffic they get and what they rank for |
| **AI Search readiness (GEO)** | Whether they appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews — most sites score an **F** here and have no idea |
| **Competitive gap** | Which competitors outrank them, and on exactly which keywords |
| **Mobile & speed** | The Core Web Vitals and mobile-speed scores Google uses to rank them |
| **Local SEO** | Whether they show up in Google map results and local searches |
| **Backlinks** | The quality of the sites linking to them |
| **Prioritized fixes** | A ranked action list — what to fix first for the biggest gain |

**The report is free to the prospect.** Your team's one goal on every call: **book a 20-minute Teams call with Ravi** to walk through what the report found for their specific site. No pitch, no cost, no obligation to them.

**The lead-in hook is AI search.** When a buyer asks ChatGPT or Google's AI "who's the best [their service] near me," most businesses don't appear at all — while a competitor does. That's brand-new, it's urgent, and almost nobody has checked it. It is the easiest reason to earn a yes.

## Before You Dial — Know These Two Things

1. **Who you want:** Owner, CEO, President, Marketing Director, VP of Marketing or Sales — whoever owns the website and the lead flow. NOT IT staff.
2. **The offer:** A free, custom SEO + AI-search visibility report on *their* website, plus a 20-minute call with Ravi to walk through it.

### One Qualifying Question

> "Can I ask — is getting found online and pulling more leads from your website something you're actively working on right now, or more on the back burner?"

**If active:** "Perfect — that's exactly who these reports help most."

**If back burner:** "Fair enough. Most owners say that until they see they're losing searches to a competitor — and now to AI answers too. The report shows exactly where. Worth 20 minutes to see yours?"

### The Ask

> "Ravi's calendar has time [THIS WEEK / NEXT WEEK]. Does [DAY] morning or [DAY] afternoon work for a 20-minute Teams call? He'll pull your report up live."

*(Two specific options — never "when are you free.")*

### Confirm and Close

> "Great — what's the best email for the invite? And quick question: what matters most to you — more traffic, more leads, or out-ranking a specific competitor? I'll pass it to Ravi so he comes prepared."

We build it specifically for your site. The 20 minutes with Ravi is just to walk you through what yours shows."

## NOTES FOR YOUR TEAM

- **AI search is the hook.** It's new, urgent, and almost nobody has checked — it's the easiest yes you'll get all week.
- **The free report is the door-opener, not the whole pitch.** Ravi covers digital growth, lead generation, and the rest of Technijian's services on the call.
- **Never quote pricing.** The report and the call are free. All investment talk happens with Ravi only.
- **Capture what they want most** — more traffic, more leads, or beating a specific competitor — at booking. It's the most useful intel you can hand Ravi.
- **Two calls max before email** — voicemail once, follow-up same day, 3-week retry.
- **The right contact owns the website and the leads** — the owner or marketing lead, not IT staff.
