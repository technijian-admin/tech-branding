## Before You Dial — Know These Two Things

1. **Who you want:** Owner, CEO, President, COO, VP of Operations, or VP of Sales. NOT IT staff or receptionists. Confirm on LinkedIn first.
2. **The offer:** A free custom AI Growth Blueprint analysis + a 20-minute strategy call. The call is where Ravi learns about their full technology and growth situation — IT, security, compliance, and AI together.

## THE SCRIPT

### Opening (10 seconds)

> "Hi [NAME] — this is [YOUR NAME] calling from Technijian, we're a technology and AI strategy firm out of Irvine. Quick question — do you have 30 seconds?"

*(If yes, continue. If "what's this about" — go to bridge.)*

---

### Bridge (20 seconds)

> "We've been working with [INDUSTRY] companies across SoCal on both the IT side — managed support, cybersecurity, compliance — and on AI strategy: specifically where AI grows revenue and cuts operational costs for their type of business. We actually just finished building a custom AI Growth Blueprint for a [INDUSTRY] company similar to [COMPANY], and I'm reaching out to see if a 20-minute conversation with our founder Ravi would make sense for you."

---

### One Qualifying Question

> "Can I ask — is technology or growth strategy something you're actively working on right now, or more on the back burner?"

**If actively looking:** "Perfect — that's exactly who these conversations are most useful for."

**If back burner:** "Fair enough. Most of the owners we speak with say the same thing — until they see the Blueprint we put together for their specific business. The 20-minute call covers both their IT situation and where AI can help. Would it be worth finding out what that looks like for [COMPANY]?"

---

### The Ask

> "Ravi's calendar has some time [THIS WEEK / NEXT WEEK]. Does [DAY] morning or [DAY] afternoon work for a 20-minute Teams call?"

*(Two specific options — never "when are you free.")*

---

### Confirm and Close

> "Great. I'll send a calendar invite — what's the best email? And quick question: what's the biggest technology or growth challenge [COMPANY] is working through right now? I'll pass it to Ravi so he comes prepared."

*(Notes go to Ravi at booking.)*

## OBJECTION HANDLERS

### "We're not interested in AI / don't need AI right now"

> "Totally understand — and this call covers more than AI. Ravi works with companies on their full technology picture: IT support, cybersecurity, compliance, and then AI on top if it makes sense for their business. A lot of the companies we talk to aren't AI-ready yet — the conversation is just as useful for understanding where your IT and security stands. Is 20 minutes worth a look?"

---

### "We already have IT covered / we have an IT person or MSP"

> "Good to know — a lot of companies we work with already have IT handled when we first speak. What we usually end up talking about is what's working well, what gaps might exist in security or compliance that their current setup doesn't cover, and whether AI is on their radar for growth. Ravi isn't going to pitch you to replace something that's working — he's going to ask a lot of questions first. Would that kind of honest conversation be worth 20 minutes?"

---

### "We don't have budget for this"

> "The strategy call itself is free — there's no cost. Ravi builds a profile of your company beforehand so you walk in with something tailored to you. Most owners find even the conversation alone surfaces a few things worth knowing. Does free work for your calendar?"

---

### "Send me something by email"

> "Absolutely — what's the best address? I'll send a short overview and follow up to make sure it landed, because Ravi's calendar books up fast. What day works for me to check back with you — [DAY] or [DAY]?"

*(Always lock in a callback date.)*

---

### "What does Technijian actually do?"

> "We're a full-service IT and AI partner for businesses in SoCal. On the IT side: managed support, cybersecurity, Microsoft 365, compliance programs for HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI — flat monthly rates, no surprise invoices. On the AI side: we build custom AI Growth Blueprints showing where AI specifically grows revenue or cuts cost for a business like yours, then we implement the pieces that make sense — AI tools, automation, lead generation. Most clients come to us for one thing and end up using several. The 20-minute call with Ravi is the fastest way to see which part is most relevant for [COMPANY]."

---

### "We're not ready to make any changes right now"

> "That's completely fine — Ravi's not going to push for an immediate change. These conversations are usually about getting a clear picture of where a company's technology stands today and what the next 12 months could look like. Most owners say it's useful even when they're not actively looking. Would that kind of no-pressure conversation be worth 20 minutes?"

## NOTES FOR YOUR TEAM

- **The AI Blueprint is the door-opener, not the whole pitch.** Use it to get the meeting. Ravi will cover IT, security, compliance, and AI in the discovery call.
- **Never quote pricing on the call.** The strategy call is free. All investment discussions happen with Ravi.
- **Pass the growth/tech challenge to Ravi at booking** — most useful intel you can capture.
- **Two calls max before email.** Voicemail once, follow-up email same day, 3-week retry.
- **The right contact is the business owner or operations leader** — not IT staff (they protect the incumbent) and not an EA (unless you're asking for a warm transfer to the decision-maker).
