### Regulatory (→ §03)
- **STB Travel Agent Licence** (Travel Agents Act 1975): General Licence = **S$100k paid-up capital + S$100k net worth**, KE required, **2-yr validity**, via GoBusiness + STB TRUST. Pacific Arena holds **TA00249**.
- **NATAS** = industry body (400+ members, ~80% of market; runs the 2 big consumer travel fairs/yr).
- **PDPA:** 10 obligations, mandatory **DPO**, **breach notice to PDPC within 3 days** if significant harm OR **≥500 individuals**, penalty cap **S$1m or 10% of SG turnover**. **PDPC restricts passport/NRIC as identifiers** (material to AI visa/passport OCR). 2024 PDPC AI advisory guidelines cover ML rec/decision systems but NOT generative AI → treat GenAI conservatively; favour in-region/PDPA-comparable vendors, minimise/tokenise PII.
- **IATA/BSP** (NewGen ISS: GoLite/GoStandard/GoGlobal) for ticketing.
