## 02 — GTM Motion: Account-Based (ABM)

**Classification:** Pure ABM — benefits brokers sell to a finite, KNOWN universe of employers.

Core Benefits' world:
- Target: OC employers with 50–500 employees experiencing healthcare cost pressure, evaluating/exiting PEOs, or needing strategic benefits architecture
- Buyers: HR Director (champion) + CFO (approver) or CEO at <75-employee accounts
- Win channel: Referrals from existing clients (#1), CPA/financial advisor referrals, PIHRA/SHRM network, LinkedIn, broker-of-record letters, PEO-exit timing
- Named-account universe: OC manufacturers, medical/professional practices, tech companies, non-profits in the 50–300 EE band

**Implication:** Growth engine framing = account intelligence + thought-leadership authority + trigger/signal monitoring (PEO exits, ACA compliance events, renewal frustration signals). NEVER "lead generation" or "shotgun" marketing — COB knows exactly who their targets are.

## 07 — Regulatory Architecture (Relevant to COB's clients)

| Framework | What It Requires | Client Pain | AI Opportunity |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|----------------|
| ACA (Affordable Care Act) | Employer mandate (50+ FTEs), 1095-C filing, PCORI fees, MLR rebates | Annual filing burden; penalty exposure | AI-automated compliance calendar; penalty risk scoring |
| ERISA | SPD maintenance, 5500 filing, fiduciary duty documentation | Few employer-HRs know what they owe | AI document review; broker-flagged gap alerts |
| COBRA | 60-day election notices, timely premium billing, admin accuracy | Manual tracking at small employers | AI-assisted COBRA admin monitoring |
| CAA 2021 (Consolidated Appropriations Act) | Broker compensation disclosure, network adequacy, reference-based pricing transparency | Disclosure requirements many brokers ignore | AI-generated compensation disclosures; machine-readable plan file automation |
| HIPAA (self-funded) | PHI handling, BAA requirements, privacy notices | Especially relevant for self-funded clients | AI-flagged HIPAA risk assessments |
