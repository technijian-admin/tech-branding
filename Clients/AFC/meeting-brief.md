## Who Abound Food Care is (research 2026-06-11 — lives in speaker notes, NOT on slides)

- Abound = contracted **compliance implementer for jurisdictions**: **Ventura County regional program** (Mar 2024; county + 8 cities) · **Garden Grove** · **Arcata** · **Sacramento Capital Food Access Alliance** (lead + grant administrator; projects through **June 30, 2026**).
