## Notes
- Tiruppur is India's knitwear capital — 4 of 5 vendors are located there
- Always request a pre-production sample before committing to bulk
- Ask for "fully landed" pricing including embroidery, not just blank polo price
- Confirm shrinkage specs (< 3% after 5 washes is the benchmark)
- For US shipment, ask about FOB vs CIF pricing and shipping timeline
