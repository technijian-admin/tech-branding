## Application Rules

When creating any Technijian-branded content:

1. **Always use the approved color palette** - Never introduce off-brand colors
2. **Use Open Sans typography** - With the specified weights and hierarchy
3. **Follow the voice guidelines** - Down-to-earth, non-corporate, clear
4. **Include the tagline** where appropriate: "technology as a solution"
5. **Reference the pod model** when discussing support/service delivery
6. **Use Core Orange for CTAs** and Core Blue for headings/links
7. **Dark backgrounds**: Use Dark Charcoal `#1A1A2E`, not pure black
8. **Never guarantee compliance outcomes** - Say "navigate" not "guarantee"
9. **Lead with client challenges**, then present solutions
10. **Avoid buzzword stacking** - No "synergistic end-to-end digital transformation"
