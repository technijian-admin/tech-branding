The Challenge

A multi-location restaurant group depended on its websites for reservation bookings, menu discovery, and local search visibility. Each location had its own site, its own content calendar, and its own set of SEMrush audit findings — but no single partner owning the whole picture.

Organic rankings were slipping on key local terms. Blog content was sporadic. Technical SEO issues (broken links, cache/cart plugin conflicts on WooCommerce-style pages, page-speed drag) were piling up in the SEMrush queue. Weekly keyword tracking and reporting existed in the spreadsheets of whoever remembered to run them.

  • SEMrush audit debt across multiple property websites
  • Sporadic blog cadence — no editorial calendar or SEO targeting
  • Technical SEO drag from cache, cart, and plugin conflicts
  • Local-term ranking slippage on reservation-driving keywords
  • No weekly reporting rhythm — rankings checked ad hoc
  • Multi-property complexity with no single accountable SEO partner
The Solution

Technijian ran the program as a monthly SEO + content + WordPress service with a 4-person specialist team: a standing weekly review cadence, monthly keyword tracking across SEMrush / Google Search Console / Google Analytics / HOTH, technical SEO issue resolution, and a consistent blog publishing pipeline tuned for local intent and property-specific pages.

  • Monthly keyword & ranking tracking across SEMrush, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, and HOTH — with expanded keyword monitoring over time
  • SEMrush / HOTH audit resolution — technical SEO issues worked down on a standing cadence across the main and test sites
  • Content production — researched, drafted, and published SEO-optimized blog posts with proper structure, keyword targeting, internal linking, and formatting
  • WordPress maintenance — cache & cart plugin optimization for stability, performance, and checkout reliability
  • Hours-of-operation accuracy across site and OpenTable for reservation-driving queries
  • Weekly client reporting and standing client calls to align priorities and review SEO improvements
